# Weekly Maintenance Procedure (LX-200)

**Document ID:** CLIN-LX200-MAINT-W1

Perform the following procedure once per week or after every 40 procedure hours, whichever is sooner.

## Tools Required

- Lint-free wipes
- Approved optical cleaning solution
- Calibrated torque driver

## Procedure

1. Power down the device and disconnect from mains.
2. Inspect the cooling reservoir level; top up with manufacturer-approved coolant if below the MIN line.
3. Inspect pump pressure indicator after cold start. Expected steady-state range: 40–90 PSI.
4. Wipe the output aperture with the approved cleaning solution.
5. Run the integrated diagnostics suite. Resolve any reported alarm before returning the device to clinical service.

## Cooling Alarm — Intermittent

If the cooling alarm triggers intermittently after extended operation (e.g. >20 minutes), inspect:
- Coolant level and air bubbles in the line
- Pump pressure trending toward the lower bound
- Ambient room temperature exceeding 25 °C

Escalate to field service if the issue recurs after a coolant top-up.
