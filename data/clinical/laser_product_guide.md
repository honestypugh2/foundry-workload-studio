# Laser System LX-200 Product Guide

**Document ID:** CLIN-LX200-001

The LX-200 is a clinical solid-state laser platform used for dermatologic and ophthalmic procedures. This guide summarizes operational parameters that clinicians should review prior to each session.

## Operational Parameters

- **Wavelength:** 1064 nm (Nd:YAG)
- **Pulse Energy Range:** 0.8–1.2 J
- **Recommended Cooling Temperature Range:** 15–28 °C
- **Pump Pressure Operating Range:** 40–90 PSI

## Pre-Procedure Checklist

1. Confirm device self-test passes without alarms.
2. Verify cooling subsystem reports a steady temperature within the recommended range.
3. Confirm pulse energy calibration is current (within 30 days).
4. Verify safety interlocks (door, key, footswitch) before activation.

## Contraindications and Warnings

- Do NOT operate if a cooling alarm is active.
- Do NOT bypass any safety interlock under any circumstance.
- Patient suitability and treatment selection are the responsibility of the treating physician.
