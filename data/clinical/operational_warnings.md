# Operational Warnings

**Document ID:** CLIN-LX200-WARN-001

The following alarm and warning conditions must be acknowledged before continuing with any patient procedure.

## Cooling Alarm

Indicates the cooling subsystem is unable to maintain the recommended 15–28 °C operating range. Immediate actions:
1. Pause the procedure.
2. Inspect coolant reservoir and pump pressure.
3. If alarm persists for >2 minutes, end the session and contact field service.

## Pulse Stability Warning

If the device reports inconsistent pulse output during use:
1. Verify pulse energy calibration date.
2. Check for ambient room temperature drift.
3. Do not increase output to compensate; recalibrate per the maintenance procedure.

## Safety Interlock Bypass

Bypassing any safety interlock is strictly prohibited and voids the device safety certification. All bypass attempts are logged and reported to Regulatory Affairs.
