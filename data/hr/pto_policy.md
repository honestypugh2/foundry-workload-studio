# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Document ID:** HR-PTO-001
**Effective:** 2025-01-01

## Eligibility

All full-time regular employees are eligible for PTO from their first day of employment. Part-time employees scheduled for 20+ hours per week accrue PTO on a prorated basis.

## Accrual

- 0–2 years of service: 15 days per year
- 3–5 years of service: 20 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 25 days per year

PTO accrues on a per-pay-period basis. Maximum carryover into the next calendar year is 5 days; balances above the cap are forfeited unless local labor law requires otherwise.

## Requesting PTO

Submit PTO requests in the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for absences of 1–3 days, and 14 days in advance for longer absences. Manager approval is required.

## Sick Time

Sick time is tracked separately from PTO. Employees receive 10 sick days per year, available immediately and not subject to carryover.
