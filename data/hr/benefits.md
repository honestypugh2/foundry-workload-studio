# Benefits Overview

**Document ID:** HR-BEN-001

## Medical, Dental, Vision

Employees may elect coverage for themselves and eligible dependents during open enrollment (November 1–30) or within 30 days of a qualifying life event. Three medical plan tiers are offered: HDHP+HSA, PPO Standard, and PPO Plus.

## Retirement (401(k))

The company matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of eligible compensation, with immediate vesting. Employees may contribute up to the IRS annual limit.

## Wellness Stipend

A $600 annual wellness stipend is reimbursable for gym memberships, fitness equipment, and approved mental wellness apps. Submit receipts via the benefits portal.

## Tuition Reimbursement

Eligible employees may receive up to $5,250 per year in tuition reimbursement for accredited programs related to their role, subject to manager pre-approval and a passing grade.
