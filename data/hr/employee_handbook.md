# Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

**Document ID:** HR-HANDBOOK-001

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to act with integrity, treat colleagues with respect, and uphold our Responsible AI principles in any interaction with company data or AI systems.

## Remote Work

Hybrid roles require a minimum of 2 days per week in-office. Fully remote roles are designated by job posting. All remote employees must use company-managed devices and follow the security baseline.

## Working Hours and Overtime

Standard working hours are 9:00–17:00 local time. Non-exempt employees must record time worked accurately and obtain manager pre-approval for overtime.

## Reporting Concerns

Concerns about safety, ethics, or compliance can be raised confidentially through the Ethics Hotline or directly to HR Business Partners. Retaliation is strictly prohibited.
