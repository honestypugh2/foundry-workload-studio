# Parental Leave Policy

**Document ID:** HR-LEAVE-002

## Paid Parental Leave

All regular employees with 6+ months of tenure are eligible for 12 weeks of fully-paid parental leave per qualifying event (birth, adoption, or foster placement). Leave must be taken within 12 months of the event and may be taken continuously or in two blocks.

## Coordination with Disability

Birthing parents may also receive short-term disability benefits in coordination with parental leave. HR Benefits will coordinate the schedule.

## Returning to Work

A formal return-to-work plan is offered, including a phased schedule for the first two weeks back. Lactation rooms are available at all primary office locations.
